Whizz Global

Whizz Global, formerly known as Travel Whizz Global, was founded in 2007 to meet the diverse travel and event-related needs of the corporate world. It has since evolved into Whizz Global, a well-known brand with service offerings in the MICE, Physical & Virtual Events, and Corporate Gifting sectors.
